Hard Hat

Keeping you Safe from those above you

The newsletter for construction workers, by construction workers

We are pro-worker; we believe that bosses profits should never come before the working-classes right to safety and a good life.

We are pro action; we believe that the working-class must struggle against the bosses and their politician mates to gain a decent living and safe conditions at work.

We are pro organisation; we believe that in order to fight the working class must be organised. On the job this is being in a union.

Our solidarity is our strength.

Our Goals

  • Inform workers on union issues.
  • Involve workers in the writing, production and distribution of this publication.
  • Reach out to non-union workers and convince them to join their union.
  • Convince union members to get active.
  • Encourage discussion amongst workers.
  • Make trouble and look good doing it!
